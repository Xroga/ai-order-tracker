Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Xroga AI Order Tracking ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and shares information about you when you use our Shopify application (the "App"). The App is developed by Muhammad Ibrahim.

We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about our data practices. This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how we use it to provide you with exceptional order tracking services powered by artificial intelligence.

Information We Collect

When you install and use Xroga AI Order Tracking, we collect the following types of information:

Shop Information

  • Shop name, domain, and contact details
  • Store owner name and email address
  • Shopify account details necessary for API access
  • Store timezone and currency settings

Order & Customer Data

  • Order details (order numbers, dates, items, prices)
  • Customer information (names, shipping addresses, contact details)
  • Shipping and fulfillment status
  • Tracking numbers and carrier information

App Usage Data

  • App configuration and settings
  • Feature usage patterns and preferences
  • Performance metrics and error logs
  • Interaction data with our AI tracking system

Important: We only collect data necessary for the app's core functionality. We never access or store sensitive payment information such as credit card numbers, which are handled exclusively by Shopify's secure payment system.

How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used exclusively to provide and improve our services:

  1. AI-Powered Order Tracking: Our artificial intelligence system analyzes order data to provide accurate tracking predictions, delivery estimates, and proactive notifications to your customers.
  2. App Functionality: To enable core features like automated tracking updates, customer notifications, and tracking page customization.
  3. Service Improvement: To enhance our AI algorithms, fix bugs, and develop new features based on usage patterns.
  4. Customer Support: To respond to your inquiries and provide technical assistance when needed.
  5. Communication: To send important updates about the app, new features, or policy changes (you can opt-out of marketing communications).
  6. Security & Compliance: To monitor for security threats, prevent fraud, and comply with legal obligations.

Data Sharing & Third Parties

We respect your data privacy and do not sell, rent, or trade your information. We only share data under the following circumstances:

With Your Consent

We may share information when you give us explicit permission to do so.

Service Providers

We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or governmental authority.

Data Security & Protection

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption: All data transmitted between our servers and Shopify is encrypted using TLS 1.2+ protocols
  • Access Control: Strict access controls and authentication mechanisms limit data access to authorized personnel only
  • Regular Audits: We conduct regular security assessments and vulnerability scans
  • Data Minimization: We only collect and retain data necessary for app functionality
  • Secure Infrastructure: Our servers are hosted on secure, enterprise-grade cloud platforms with firewall protection

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information below.

When you uninstall the app, we initiate data deletion processes within 30 days, except where we need to retain certain information for legal, regulatory, or legitimate business purposes.
